## SECTION: WORKFLOW OVERVIEW

### Headline
From job description to hiring decision — in one workflow.

### Layout
Horizontal step-by-step flow (desktop) / vertical stacked (mobile)

### Steps

**Step 1 — Create your role**
Paste in a job description or build one from scratch. QualifyMe maps the role to the cognitive and technical demands it actually requires.

**Step 2 — Generate your assessment**
The platform builds a customized assessment — MCQs, coding challenges, video Q&A, or a cognitive simulation — matched to the role. Edit, adjust, or approve as-is.

**Step 3 — Invite candidates**
Send assessments via branded portal. Candidates complete on any device, at their own pace (within your set window).

**Step 4 — Review ranked results**
AI evaluates every response and delivers a ranked candidate list with detailed breakdowns. No manual scoring. No rubric wrangling.

**Step 5 — Go deeper when it matters** *(Simulations tier)*
For final-round candidates, run a cognitive simulation. Get a structured hiring recommendation backed by every action they took in the scenario.
