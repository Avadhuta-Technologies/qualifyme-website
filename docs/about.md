## SECTION: OUR BELIEF

### Headline

The hiring system is broken at its core.

### Body

For decades, hiring decisions have relied on indirect signals:

* Resumes that reflect how well someone presents themselves
* Interviews that reward confidence and storytelling
* Tests that measure recall, not real-world judgment

These methods don’t answer the most important question:
**Can this person actually do the job?**

We believe hiring should be grounded in **observable performance**, not proxies.
