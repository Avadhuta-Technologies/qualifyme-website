## Case Study: How SoftTech Engineers Limited Scaled Screening Across Regions

### Overview

**Company:** SoftTech Engineers Limited
**Website:** SoftTech Engineers Limited
**Industry:** Engineering Software & Technology
**Team Size:** 600+ employees

**Hiring Characteristics:**

* Multi-region hiring across India
* **20+ active job openings** at any given time
* Candidate inflow from:

  * Naukri
  * Recruitment agencies
  * Other sourcing channels

---

### The Challenge: Distributed Hiring, Slower Screening

With multiple roles open simultaneously and candidates coming from various sources, SoftTech faced:

* **High volume of resumes across roles and regions**
* Lack of a **standardized screening mechanism**
* Delays in shortlisting candidates due to **manual filtering**
* Difficulty maintaining **consistent evaluation quality** across different hiring teams

This resulted in:

* Slower movement from application → interview
* Increased dependency on manual effort
* Risk of **missing strong candidates due to delays**

---

### The Solution: Centralized Screening with QualifyMe

SoftTech Engineers implemented **QualifyMe** as a unified screening layer across all roles.

**What changed:**

* All candidates, regardless of source, were routed through **role-based assessments**
* Screening became **standardized across regions and teams**
* Recruiters received **pre-qualified candidate pools** instead of raw resumes

---

### The Impact: Faster, Consistent Hiring Pipeline

After adopting QualifyMe:

* Screening time was **significantly reduced across all roles**
* Hiring teams could **quickly shortlist candidates** based on performance
* Evaluation became **consistent**, independent of source or region
* Recruiters and hiring managers focused only on **qualified candidates**

---

### Key Outcomes

* **Faster screening cycles** across 20+ active roles
* **Standardized evaluation process** across regions
* Reduced manual dependency on resume filtering
* Improved ability to **handle large-scale hiring efficiently**

---

### Why It Worked

QualifyMe introduced a **centralized, performance-driven screening layer** that worked across:

* Multiple job roles
* Multiple geographies
* Multiple candidate sources

Instead of fragmented and manual processes, SoftTech achieved:

* Uniform candidate evaluation
* Faster decision-making
* Scalable hiring operations

---

### Conclusion

For a large, distributed organization like SoftTech Engineers, QualifyMe enabled:

* Scalable hiring across regions
* Faster and more reliable screening
* Consistency in candidate quality

This case highlights how a **centralized assessment layer** can bring **speed, structure, and scalability** to enterprise hiring workflows.
