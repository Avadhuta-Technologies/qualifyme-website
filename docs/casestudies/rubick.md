## Case Study: How RubickAI Streamlined Fresher Hiring and Reduced On-Ground Effort

### Overview

**Company:** RubickAI
**Website:** RubickAI
**Industry:** AI-powered Retail Ecosystem

**Platform Capabilities:**

* Multi-brand and seller management
* Marketplace selling
* Catalog automation
* 60+ real-time market & competitor intelligence data points
* Built-in growth automation (Rubick OS Suite)

**Hiring Focus:**

* Freshers from Tier-2 cities

---

### The Challenge: High Effort, Offline Hiring Bottleneck

RubickAI followed a **resume-first, offline assessment approach**:

* Candidates shortlisted from resumes were **called to the office**
* Assessments were conducted **physically, on-site**
* Engineers had to:

  * Coordinate candidate communication
  * Organize and supervise assessments
  * Spend time evaluating large batches

Key issues:

* **High operational overhead** for every hiring cycle
* **Significant engineering time diverted** to logistics instead of product work
* Inefficient filtering — many candidates invited were **not truly qualified**

---

### The Solution: Moving Screening Online with QualifyMe

RubickAI implemented **QualifyMe** to automate their initial screening process.

**What changed:**

* Candidates completed **assessments remotely**
* No need to call applicants to the office for initial evaluation
* Only top performers were shortlisted for interviews

---

### The Impact: Leaner Funnel, Faster Hiring

With QualifyMe in place:

* From **50+ applicants**, only **~5 candidates qualified**
* Engineers no longer needed to manage **on-ground assessments**
* Interviews were conducted only for **high-potential candidates**
* One candidate was **quickly selected and hired**

---

### Key Outcomes

* **90% reduction** in candidates reaching interview stage
* Eliminated need for **offline assessment logistics**
* **Significant reduction in engineering effort**
* Faster hiring decisions with **better candidate quality**

---

### Why It Worked

QualifyMe replaced a **manual, location-dependent process** with a **scalable, automated evaluation layer**.

Instead of bringing candidates in and then filtering:

* Candidates were filtered **before any physical interaction**
* Engineers engaged only when **it truly mattered**

---

### Conclusion

For companies like RubickAI hiring at scale from distributed locations, QualifyMe enabled:

* Remote-first hiring workflows
* Reduced operational complexity
* Faster and more efficient hiring decisions

This case shows how shifting from **offline assessments to automated screening** can dramatically improve both **efficiency and hiring outcomes**.
