## Case Study: How VNurt Technology Solutions Improved Candidate Quality and Increased Placement Success

### Overview

**Company:** VNurt Technology Solutions
**Website:** VNurt Technology Solutions
**Industry:** HR Sourcing & Software Services
**Experience:** 10+ years

**Core Services:**

* Talent sourcing and placement for client companies
* Internal hiring
* Software development services

---

### The Challenge: High Stakes, Low Margin for Error

VNurt operates in a **performance-driven recruitment model**:

* Revenue is generated **only when a role is successfully closed**
* Poor candidate quality directly leads to **lost deals and wasted effort**

Key issues in their hiring workflow:

* Heavy reliance on **manual interviews to ensure quality**
* **Time-intensive screening process** across multiple roles
* Delays in sending candidates to clients, leading to **missed opportunities**
* High effort with **uncertain outcomes**

This created a bottleneck:

> More effort did not guarantee better results — but delays guaranteed lost deals.

---

### The Solution: Automating Quality Screening with QualifyMe

VNurt integrated **QualifyMe** into their recruitment workflow across all roles.

Instead of manually filtering candidates upfront:

* Every candidate went through **automated assessments**
* Only candidates who demonstrated **real capability** were shortlisted
* Recruiters focused only on **high-quality, pre-qualified candidates**

---

### The Impact: Faster Closures, Better Candidates

After implementing QualifyMe:

* **Low-quality candidates were filtered out early**
* Shortlisting became **significantly faster**
* Only **assessment-qualified candidates** were shared with clients
* Reduced dependency on **manual interviews for initial screening**

---

### Key Outcomes

* **Higher candidate quality** submitted to clients
* **Faster turnaround time** for candidate shortlisting
* **Increased deal success rate**
* **Revenue growth** due to more successful placements
* Improved **client satisfaction and trust**

---

### Why It Worked

QualifyMe introduced a **consistent, performance-based filtering layer** before human intervention.

Instead of relying on subjective judgment early in the process, VNurt could:

* Objectively evaluate all candidates at scale
* Prioritize only those who meet role expectations
* Move faster without compromising on quality

---

### Conclusion

For a recruitment-driven business like VNurt, where **quality directly impacts revenue**, QualifyMe enabled:

* Scalable screening without increasing team effort
* Faster and more reliable candidate delivery
* Stronger client outcomes and repeat business

This case highlights how **automated, performance-based assessments** can directly translate into **higher revenue and better customer satisfaction** in staffing businesses.
