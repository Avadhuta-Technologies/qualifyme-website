## Case Study: How Avadhuta Technologies Reduced Hiring Noise and Closed Roles Faster

### Overview

**Company:** Avadhuta Technologies
**Industry:** Software Services
**Founded:** 2017
**Key Hiring Roles:**

* Frontend Developers (React.js, Vue.js, Angular)
* Backend Developers (MERN, Java, Python stacks)
* Sales Representatives
* Software Testing Professionals

---

### The Challenge: High Volume, Low Signal

Avadhuta Technologies relied on platforms like Naukri and LinkedIn for hiring.

However, their hiring funnel had a critical inefficiency:

* Each job opening attracted **250–300 applications**
* Only **~40% of candidates were actually qualified**
* **Manual resume screening required founder involvement**
* Engineers were spending significant time interviewing **unfit candidates**

This created two major problems:

1. **Wasted engineering bandwidth** on low-quality interviews
2. **Slow hiring cycles**, impacting delivery timelines

---

### The Solution: Screening with QualifyMe

Avadhuta Technologies implemented **QualifyMe** as the first step in their hiring process.

Instead of relying on resumes, candidates were evaluated through **role-specific assessments** designed to measure actual capability.

**What changed:**

* Every applicant was required to complete a QualifyMe assessment
* Only top-performing candidates were shortlisted for interviews
* Interview decisions were based on **performance, not claims**

---

### The Impact: Drastic Reduction in Hiring Noise

Across multiple roles, QualifyMe delivered immediate results:

* Only **5–10% of applicants qualified** after assessment
* Engineers interviewed **far fewer, but highly relevant candidates**
* **Founder involvement in screening was eliminated**

#### Example: Software Testing Fresher Role

* Total Applicants: **450**
* Candidates Qualified via QualifyMe: **~20**
* Final Interviews Conducted: **4**
* Role Closure: **Successfully hired from top performers**

---

### Key Outcomes

* **90%+ reduction** in candidates reaching interview stage
* **Significant time savings** for engineering teams
* **Faster hiring cycles**
* **Higher confidence in candidate quality**
* Engineers spent more time on **productive work instead of filtering candidates**

---

### Why It Worked

QualifyMe shifted hiring from **resume-based filtering → performance-based selection**.

Instead of asking *“Does this candidate look good on paper?”*
Avadhuta Technologies could now answer:
**“Can this candidate actually perform the job?”**

---

### Conclusion

For a growing software services company like Avadhuta Technologies, where engineering time is critical, QualifyMe enabled:

* Precision hiring
* Efficient use of internal resources
* Faster and better hiring decisions

This case demonstrates how replacing manual screening with structured assessments can transform hiring outcomes at scale.
